Why Outsource IT

Your IT department, without the headcount

For a growing Los Angeles business, technology is mission-critical — but building an internal IT department to support it is expensive, slow, and hard to staff. Hiring even one qualified IT person means a six-figure salary, benefits, recruiting costs, and the risk that a single hire can't cover everything from cybersecurity to cloud to networking. When that person is on vacation or out sick, your support disappears. Outsourcing solves this by giving you an entire team of specialists for a predictable monthly fee.

Outsourced IT — also called managed IT services — means Pro Link Systems becomes your IT department. We proactively monitor, secure, and maintain your systems; our 24/7 help desk answers when your team needs help; and we plan your technology roadmap so IT supports where your business is headed. You get enterprise-grade tools, security, and expertise that would be impossible to assemble in-house at the same price — all under one flat monthly rate with no long-term contract.

The math is straightforward. A single in-house IT manager in Los Angeles costs well over $100,000 a year once you factor in salary, benefits, and overhead — and you still only get one person, working business hours, with one set of skills. Outsourced IT typically costs a fraction of that while delivering broader coverage, faster response, and round-the-clock availability. It's why so many LA companies with 5 to 100 employees choose to outsource. Explore our full range of IT services to see everything included.
