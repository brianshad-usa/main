The Real Cost of Downtime

In Logistics, Every Minute
of Downtime Has a Price.

When IT fails in a warehouse or fleet operation, the costs compound fast — idle labor, missed dock appointments, detention fees, and customer SLA penalties.

Warehouse Wi-Fi Dead Zones

When RF scanners lose signal in the racks, picking stops. Workers walk to find coverage, scans get missed, and inventory accuracy degrades — all while labor costs keep running.

Broken EDI Feeds Stall Shipments

When an EDI connection to a major retailer fails, ASNs don't transmit, appointments get missed, and chargebacks pile up. By the time anyone notices, the damage is done.

Ransomware Targeting Supply Chains

Logistics is a top ransomware target — attackers know a frozen WMS halts the entire operation. One successful attack can shut down a distribution center for days.
