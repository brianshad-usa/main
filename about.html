Our Story

Built from the ground up in Woodland Hills

Pro Link Systems was founded in 1999 by Brian Shad with a straightforward mission: help small and midsize businesses in Southern California get more out of technology. At the time, most IT vendors were transactional — they sold you hardware, collected a check, and disappeared. Brian saw an opportunity to do things differently.

From a single office in Woodland Hills, Pro Link grew into a full-service managed IT and cybersecurity partner serving businesses across the greater Los Angeles area. What began as break-fix support has evolved into proactive, strategic IT management — because the problems businesses face today are fundamentally different from what they faced in 1999.

Ransomware didn't exist the way it does now. Remote work wasn't a default. AI wasn't reshaping job descriptions. Cloud wasn't the infrastructure backbone it is today. Pro Link has grown alongside these shifts — adapting our services, building new competencies, and staying ahead of what our clients need next.

Twenty-five years in, we're still independently owned, still headquartered in Woodland Hills, and still operating on the same principle: technology should move your business forward, not hold it back.

How We Work

Our approach to managed IT

Good IT management is proactive, not reactive. Here's how we structure every client engagement to make sure problems get prevented, not just patched.

Assess

Every engagement begins with a thorough assessment of your current environment. We document your infrastructure, identify risks, and build a clear picture of your baseline — before we recommend anything.

Stabilize

We fix what's broken, patch what's vulnerable, and establish monitoring across your environment. Most new clients see a noticeable improvement in stability within the first 30 days.

Optimize

Once stable, we start optimizing — right-sizing cloud spend, improving performance, tightening security, and building a technology roadmap that aligns with your business goals for the next 1–3 years.
