The Healthcare IT Problem

Healthcare IT Failures
Put Patients at Risk — Not Just Data.

Healthcare organizations are the #1 ransomware target in the US. An EHR outage, a breached medical device, or a missed HIPAA control doesn't just create liability — it directly affects the quality of patient care. Generic IT providers don't understand what's at stake.

Ransomware Targeting Patient Records

Healthcare ransomware attacks have tripled since 2020. A single successful attack can encrypt your entire EHR system, force you to paper-based care, and trigger a mandatory HIPAA breach notification.

Unsecured Connected Medical Devices

IoT infusion pumps, imaging systems, and patient monitors are often unpatched and flat-networked with clinical workstations. A compromised device can serve as an entry point into your entire network.

HIPAA Violations Without an IT Partner Who Knows Healthcare

Audit log gaps, unencrypted workstations, and missing access controls are among the most common HIPAA findings. Most IT providers don't know what OCR is looking for. We do.
