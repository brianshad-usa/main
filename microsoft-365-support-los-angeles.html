Why a Local M365 Partner

Microsoft 365, done properly — and kept that way

Microsoft 365 runs most Los Angeles businesses: email, Teams meetings, shared files, and the apps your team lives in every day. But there's a big gap between simply having Microsoft 365 and having it set up, secured, and managed well. Misconfigured tenants, unsecured accounts, sprawling SharePoint sites, and wasted licensing are everywhere — usually because the platform was set up once and never properly maintained. Pro Link Systems closes that gap.

We handle the full Microsoft 365 lifecycle. If you're moving from on-premises Exchange, Google Workspace, or an older host, we plan and execute the migration with little or no downtime. Once you're live, we manage Exchange and email, Teams, SharePoint and OneDrive, and Azure AD / Entra ID identities, and we provide a help desk your staff can reach any time. As part of our broader cloud services and managed IT services, your Microsoft 365 environment stays current, organized, and aligned with how your business actually works.

Security is where most Microsoft 365 deployments fall short. Out of the box, critical protections are turned off, leaving accounts exposed to the phishing and business email compromise attacks that hit LA companies daily. We harden your tenant with multi-factor authentication, conditional access, and advanced threat protection as part of our cybersecurity services — and we right-size your licensing so you're not overpaying for seats or features you don't use. The result is Microsoft 365 that's faster, safer, and a better value.
