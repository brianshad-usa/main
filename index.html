ProLink also built a custom application enabling hotel charges to post directly to guest folios, streamlining revenue management and front-desk operations.

Pro Link Systems offers flat-rate managed IT pricing tailored to the size and needs of your business. Most LA companies with 5–50 employees pay between $75–150 per user per month. Contact us for a free assessment and custom quote.

No. Pro Link Systems does not require long-term contracts. We earn your business every month through results, not lock-in agreements.

Yes. We support HIPAA, PCI DSS, CMMC, and SOC 2 compliance for clients in healthcare, legal, financial, and defense sectors throughout Los Angeles and Southern California.

How fast do you respond to IT issues?

Pro Link Systems engineers respond to support requests in under 15 minutes on average, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You reach a real engineer, not a ticket queue or offshore call center.
