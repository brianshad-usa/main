The Real Risks

Real Estate IT Challenges
That Cost Firms Dearly.

From wire fraud to agent device chaos, LA real estate firms face IT challenges that directly impact transactions, client trust, and the bottom line.

Wire Fraud During Transaction Close

Attackers monitor real estate email threads, wait for closing, then send fraudulent wire instructions impersonating the escrow officer or listing agent. The average real estate BEC loss exceeds $350,000 per incident — and recovery is rare once the wire clears.

Unsecured Agent Devices & Personal Email

Agents working from personal phones, personal laptops, and personal email accounts create massive security gaps. When an agent's personal Gmail is compromised, so is every transaction thread they've ever had.

Property Management Software Downtime

When AppFolio or Yardi goes down at month-end, rent collection stalls and maintenance tickets pile up. Property managers can't afford IT that doesn't prioritize their PM platform as a business-critical system.
