The Operational Reality

Waste IT Challenges
Most Providers Don't Understand.

Waste and recycling operations have IT needs unlike any other industry — and generic IT providers don't grasp what's at stake when a scale house or routing system goes down.

Scale House Downtime Backs Up the Gate

When the weigh scale system fails at a transfer station, trucks back up at the gate, tipping stops, and revenue capture halts. Every minute of scale downtime is trucks idling and drivers waiting.

Routing & Billing Disconnects Lose Revenue

When what happens on the route doesn't flow correctly into billing, services go unbilled and disputes multiply. Broken routing-to-billing integration quietly bleeds revenue every single day.

Ransomware Targeting Critical Infrastructure

Waste management is designated critical infrastructure — and a growing ransomware target. An attack that freezes routing and billing can paralyze collection across an entire service area.
