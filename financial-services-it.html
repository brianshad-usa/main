What Keeps You Up at Night

The IT Risks Financial
Firms Can't Ignore.

Financial services firms are one of the most targeted industries for cyberattacks — and one of the most heavily scrutinized by regulators. You need IT that addresses both simultaneously.

Business Email Compromise & Wire Fraud

Financial firms lose more to BEC than any other industry. Attackers compromise email accounts, impersonate executives, and redirect wire transfers. The average loss exceeds $120,000 per incident.

Regulatory Examination with No Documentation

SEC and FINRA examiners now routinely ask for your Written Information Security Policy, incident response plan, access control logs, and vendor oversight program. If it doesn't exist on paper, it doesn't exist.

Ransomware Targeting Financial Data

Client financial records, tax documents, and investment data are high-value ransomware targets. A single successful attack can trigger client notification obligations, regulatory action, and reputational damage that takes years to recover from.
