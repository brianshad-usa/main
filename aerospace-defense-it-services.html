Compliance Expertise

The Regulatory Frameworks

Defense contractors face overlapping compliance requirements that demand IT partners who understand the specifics — not partners who Google acronyms after the contract is signed.

CMMC 2.0

Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification

Required for all DoD contractors handling Federal Contract Information (FCI) or Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI). We implement the full NIST SP 800-171 control set and prepare your documentation for C3PAO assessment.

  • Full gap assessment against 110 NIST 800-171 practices
  • System Security Plan (SSP) development
  • Plan of Action & Milestones (POA&M)
  • C3PAO assessment preparation & evidence collection
  • Continuous monitoring post-certification
ITAR / EAR

Export Control-Aware IT Infrastructure

ITAR and the Export Administration Regulations extend directly to how you store, transmit, and control access to technical data. We build IT environments that reflect your export control obligations — not ones that create violations.

  • Access controls preventing unauthorized foreign national access
  • Network segmentation between ITAR & non-ITAR environments
  • Encrypted storage & transmission of controlled technical data
  • Audit logging aligned to Technology Control Plan requirements
  • Cloud service evaluation for ITAR cloud data
DFARS / FAR

Contract Clause IT Requirements

DFARS 252.204-7012 (Safeguarding Covered Defense Information) and FAR 52.204-21 (Basic Safeguarding of Covered Contractor Information Systems) impose specific technical and reporting requirements on defense contractor IT systems.

  • Cloud service provider review (FedRAMP compliance)
  • Cyber incident reporting capability (72-hour requirement)
  • Media preservation & protection protocols
  • Subcontractor IT security flow-down management
  • Annual self-assessment scoring documentation
