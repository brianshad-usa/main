Yes. The Children's Internet Protection Act (CIPA) requires schools and libraries that receive E-Rate funding to filter or block obscene and harmful content and to monitor minors' online activity. We deploy and manage CIPA-compliant content filtering across your network and student devices, maintain the documentation and acceptable use policies CIPA requires, and configure filtering that protects students without getting in the way of legitimate educational use. We also help ensure your filtering satisfies the requirements tied to E-Rate eligibility.
Student records are among the most sensitive data any organization holds, and FERPA — along with California's SOPIPA and AB 1584 — sets strict requirements for how they're protected and who can access them. We implement role-based access controls so only authorized staff can see student records, encrypt data on devices and in transit, secure your student information system (SIS), vet the ed-tech vendors that handle student data, and maintain the data privacy documentation these laws require. We build student data protection into your IT environment from the ground up.

We support the full classroom technology stack — 1:1 device programs (Chromebooks, iPads, laptops), mobile device management (MDM) to deploy, secure, and manage student devices at scale, interactive displays and projectors, and the classroom Wi-Fi that has to handle hundreds of devices at once. We handle device enrollment, app deployment, content filtering on devices, and the summer refresh cycles when schools roll out new equipment — so technology supports learning instead of disrupting it.

We help you make the most of available funding — including the federal E-Rate program, which provides discounts of 20 to 90 percent on eligible internet, networking, and telecommunications costs for schools and libraries. We also help education nonprofits access discounted and donated technology through programs like Google for Education, Microsoft 365 Education, and TechSoup, stretching every dollar further toward your mission.
